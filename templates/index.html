Sub-resource integrity is a new specification which allows web developers to ensure that the third-party resources they load match a particular hash.

Generate a third-party resource

Enter the URL of the sub-resource you wish to include on your site:

Alternatively, you can use the following command-line to generate a hash manually:

cat file.js | openssl dgst -sha256 -binary | openssl enc -base64 -A | sed -e 's/+/-/g' -e 's/\//_/g' -e 's/=*$//g'

Test your browser

To verify whether or not your browser supports sub-resource integrity, open this test page.

If it doesn't, download the latest version of Firefox or Chrome and try again.
